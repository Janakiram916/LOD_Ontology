LOD Ontology Specification

Abstract

LODO is an ontology that incorporates multiple LOD specifications based on respective industry standards. Its purpose is to facilitate the representation of LOD-sensitive BIM data through formal ontology definitions. This approach can be readily customized and implemented in the current BIM process, as it allows for the representation of BIM data in various ontologies such as BOT, ifcOWL, and others.
